The Client Who we worked with

Government health institutions (national, provincial, and city levels), including maternal & child health centers (posyandu), public health centers (puskesmas), and policy makers responsible for stunting reduction programs.

02

Problems The main obstacles we faced

  • Health data is still recorded manually.
  • Stunting detection is slow and not data-driven.
  • Online consultation is limited by distance and cost.
  • Government struggles to access timely data for policymaking.
  • Monitoring of maternal and child health is not integrated.
    • 03

    Solutions How we solved the challenges

  • Digitalizing maternal and child health data into a real-time system.
  • Automated stunting detection supported by visual analytics.
  • Providing online consultation features for maternal and child health.
  • Integrating health information to support faster, accurate policymaking.
  • Offering additional features such as pregnancy monitoring, medication bank, child & mother guide, AI assistant, and family monitoring.
