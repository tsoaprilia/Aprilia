Exvecto: POS & Inventory Management

Redesign and development of an integrated Point of Sale (POS) and inventory management application, specifically tailored to meet the unique needs of clothing printing shops, such as custom T-shirts, hoodies, hats, and merchandise.

Date

2023

Client

Exvecto

Category

UI/UX Design & POS System

Tantangan & Solusi Proyek

01

Client About the Printing Shop

Exvecto is a service business for printing and selling custom clothing (T-shirts, hoodies, hats) and merchandise. They required a system to efficiently manage rapid sales processes (POS), custom orders, and the stock of raw materials (blank T-shirts, ink, etc.).

02

Problem Key Challenges Faced

The main challenges were managing dual inventory (finished goods and raw materials), complex sales reports due to custom orders, and transaction speed at the cashier. The old system did not support product tracking by design nor non-cash payments (barcode).

03

Solusi Implemented Design

  • Detailed Inventory Management: Separating the stock of raw materials and finished products, including a recipe/component feature for custom orders.
  • Interactive Sales Charts: Providing a dashboard with daily/monthly sales charts to visually monitor the shop's performance.
  • Fast Barcode Payment & POS: Integrating a barcode scanning feature for efficient payment and product recording, speeding up the checkout process at the cashier.
